# House rules — D&D 5e

The written deviations from SRD rules-as-written. Per the binder's law: **a ruling that isn't written down doesn't exist** — if it's not on this page, the SRD applies.

Improvised rulings made mid-session are not house rules yet; they land in the session recap's notes and get promoted here (or dropped) in post-session review.

## Active house rules

_None yet. The SRD applies in full._

## Template for adding one

```markdown
### <short name>
- **Rule:** what applies at this table, stated completely.
- **Replaces/modifies:** the SRD rule it deviates from.
- **Why:** one line.
- **Since:** <yyyy-mm-dd>, <origin: session ruling / owner decision>
```
