# SRD quick-reference: ability checks

Source: D&D 5e SRD 5.1. This file summarizes rules-as-written; it contains no house rules (those live in `rules/dnd5e/house-rules.md`).

## The core roll

d20 + ability modifier (+ proficiency bonus if proficient in an applicable skill or tool) vs. a Difficulty Class (DC). Equal or exceed the DC = success.

| Task difficulty | DC |
|---|---|
| Very easy | 5 |
| Easy | 10 |
| Medium | 15 |
| Hard | 20 |
| Very hard | 25 |
| Nearly impossible | 30 |

## Ability modifiers

Modifier = (score − 10) / 2, rounded down. (10–11 → +0, 12–13 → +1, 8–9 → −1, etc.)

## Advantage and disadvantage

- Advantage: roll 2d20, take the **higher**. Disadvantage: roll 2d20, take the **lower**.
- Multiple sources don't stack — you either have it or you don't.
- If you have both advantage and disadvantage from any sources, they cancel: roll **one** d20 (regardless of how many of each).

## Skills by ability

- **Strength:** Athletics
- **Dexterity:** Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
- **Intelligence:** Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, Religion
- **Wisdom:** Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Perception, Survival
- **Charisma:** Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion

(Constitution has no associated skills.)

## Contests

Both parties roll an ability check; higher total wins. A tie means the situation stays as it was.

## Group checks

When the whole party attempts something together, everyone rolls; if **at least half** succeed, the group succeeds.

## Passive checks

Passive score = 10 + all modifiers that would apply to the roll (+5 with advantage, −5 with disadvantage). Used when no roll is made — most often **passive Perception** to notice hidden things.

## Working together

A creature can **help** another (see the Help action in combat; outside combat, helping grants advantage where cooperation plausibly helps and the helper could attempt the task alone).

## Saving throws

d20 + ability modifier (+ proficiency bonus if proficient in that save) vs. a DC set by the effect. Spell save DC = 8 + caster's proficiency bonus + spellcasting ability modifier.
